Our Services

Exterior Detailing

Our comprehensive exterior detailing includes washing, waxing, polishing, and UV protection for your boat's hull and topsides. We use marine-grade products that protect against saltwater, sun damage, and environmental elements.

Interior Detailing

From cabin cleaning to upholstery treatment, our interior detailing service covers every aspect of your boat's living spaces. We'll remove stains, eliminate odors, and apply protective treatments to vinyl, leather, and fabric surfaces.

Metal Polishing

Restore the shine to your boat's metal surfaces with our specialized metal polishing service. We'll remove oxidation, tarnish, and water spots from stainless steel, chrome, aluminum, and brass fixtures.

Teak Restoration

Our teak restoration service brings back the natural beauty of your wooden surfaces. We clean, sand, and apply protective oils or sealants to preserve and enhance the appearance of your teak decking and trim.

Hull Maintenance

Keep your hull in top condition with our regular maintenance services. We'll remove algae, barnacles, and other marine growth, and apply anti-fouling treatments to protect against future buildup.

Paint Correction

Our paint correction services remove scratches, oxidation, and fading from your boat's gelcoat or painted surfaces. We use professional-grade compounds and equipment to restore the original finish.
